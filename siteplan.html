W05 Project: Site Plan

Site Name

NomaShop — This name was selected because it represents a modern, accessible online shopping platform offering phones, electronics, fashion, and more. It is short, memorable, and relevant to the project theme.

Optional domain availability: nomashop.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of NomaShop is to provide a hub for online shopping where users can browse products, compare prices, and purchase items conveniently. The site will feature categories such as phones, electronics, fashion, and home appliances, along with a secure checkout process.

Scenarios

  • Which smartphones are currently trending and available at affordable prices?
  • How can I quickly access my shopping cart and proceed to checkout?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: Orange (#ff7b00) — used for headings, buttons, and highlights.

Secondary Color: Deep Blue (#004466) — used for header, footer, and background accents.

This document itself uses these colors for headings and layout to demonstrate the scheme.

Typography

Roboto: used for body text, paragraphs, and lists for readability.

Playfair Display: used for headings to provide a strong, elegant contrast.
