MyShop Site Plan

Site Name

MyShop – Chosen because it is a simple, memorable name that communicates the purpose of an online shopping platform clearly.

Site Purpose

The purpose of MyShop is to provide a convenient online platform for users to browse, search, and purchase a variety of products such as electronics, clothing, and jewelry. The site will also offer features such as wishlists, shopping carts, and product quick views.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: Sarah, a college student, wants to find affordable electronics for her dorm. She uses the search feature to filter products by category and price.
  • Scenario 2: John, a busy professional, wants to quickly buy a gift for his wife. He uses the wishlist and cart functionality to complete the purchase efficiently.

Color Schema

The site uses the following primary and secondary colors:

Primary Blue – used for buttons, headers, and active category highlights.
Red – used for cart notifications, important alerts, and error messages.

Typography

The site uses Arial, sans-serif as the main font for body content, headings, and navigation to ensure readability and a clean modern look. Headings (h1, h2, h3) use bold styling to distinguish sections.
